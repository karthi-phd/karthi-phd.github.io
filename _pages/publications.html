Over the past two decades, my research has focused on bridging computational prediction with biological validation to understand how small molecules influence disease networks. These efforts have resulted in a growing body of peer-reviewed publications across infectious disease therapeutics, cardioprotection, molecular modeling, and systems pharmacology.

A complete and automatically updated list is available on Google Scholar.
